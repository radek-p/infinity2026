The workshop will take place in person at **Royal Holloway, University of London, UK**, as a satellite workshop of **ICALP 2026**.
Royal Holloway is located in Egham, Surrey, which is approximately 20 miles (40 minutes by rail) from central London.

The campus is a 15-20 minute walk from Egham station, which has regular train services from London Waterloo and Reading.
For guidance on travel and accommodation, as well as local information and suggested hotels, we recommend checking the [main ICALP website](https://icalppodcspaa2026.cs.rhul.ac.uk/local/).
