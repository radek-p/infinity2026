The workshop will take place in person at **Royal Holloway, University of London, UK**, as a satellite workshop of **ICALP 2026**.
Royal Holloway is located in Egham, Surrey, which is approximately 20 miles (40 minutes by train) from central London.

The campus is a 15-20 minute walk from Egham station, which has regular services from London Waterloo and Reading.
Practical guidance on travel and accommodation will be updated closer to the event. For now, we recommend checking the [main ICALP website](https://icalppodcspaa2026.cs.rhul.ac.uk/local/) for general local info and suggested hotels in the Egham and Windsor area.
